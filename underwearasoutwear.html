LOOKBOOK: Underwear as outerwear


May 12, 2018
Whenever my mum sees me in a top like this the first reaction is always “Is that a pyjama top? Why are you wearing a pyjama top outside?”. “Yes mum, but it is fashionable to wear this as outerwear” - I always try helplessly to explain myself.. No matter how many times we have this conversation, she is never left convinced. But I personally love this contrasting style of the bold black jeans and heels and the softness of the satin material underneath. It gives the outfit such an elegance.
