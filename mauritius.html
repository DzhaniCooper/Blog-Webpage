Travel Blog: Mauritius


January 6, 2018
This blog post has been a long time in the making, but I just never quite got round to it. Mauritius was such a magical place, beyond all of my expectations and dreams. It was a gift for our honeymoon, and I was left in awe by all the beauty, kindness and exotic surroundings.




I had never been on such a long flight before- it took us 12 hours to get there!! But oh my was it worth it. The resort was called the Sugar Beach Resort, which was part of a larger network of hotels, and we were allowed to roam freely between all of them and use all the facilities. There were a few different restaurants to choose from to eat in during the day, and different performances during dinner time in the main restaurant.






Normally all I want to do at the beach is sunbathe, but it was such a peaceful place that I spent most of the time in the shade, just relaxing and reading books. I spent my time there reading How to be a Badass by Jen Sincero, which I highly recommend! It really inspired me to look at life in a different way, and would be useful to anyone.




The resort also provided a wide range of free water sports activities, and in the day when the weather was a little bit cloudier we took full advantage of them. We enjoyed kayaking the most, but also went on the paddle boat.




It was beautiful honeymoon destination that I would recommend to anyone, and the hotel made it extra special by organizing a romantic dinner by a beach fire on one of our nights there.






Until next time,
