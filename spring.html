Spring Sunshine


April 22, 2018
The weather has been so amazing in London these past few days. I felt like I was on a mini holiday abroad. Everywhere I went the parks were full of people trying to enjoy every sun rays.


The beautiful sunshine definitely inspired this whole outfit. I was going around the shops trying to start updating my wardrobe for Spring/Summer 18, and when I saw this dress I was head over heels.. I even bought it without trying it on first! Talk about love at first sight!




I am obsessed with this shade of yellow- it reminds me of warm evening by the beach with a cocktail in one hand, or a weekend barbecue with delicious food with friends!






So effortlessly chic, and can be dressed up with heels as I have done, or dressed down with a white t-shirt underneath and a pair of sneakers. Super cool and comfy!




I had also been on the look out for a circle straw bag- they have been all over Instagram and I was obsessed! So when I saw this beauty in Primark, I couldn’t believe my eyes! The quality wayyyy exceeds the price (as is very often the case with Primani). My mum even quickly started eyeing it up and asking to borrow it.




P.S. I am around 6 months pregnant in these photos, and unless I’m standing sideways you can hardly tell!
